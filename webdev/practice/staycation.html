Stargazer's Favorite Staycation: Goblin Valley

Goblin Valley state park, located in Emery County, is one of Utah's many state parks. Located 126 miles away (just over 3 hours) from Provo lies Goblin valley, a mini Bryce Caynon. It got is name from the rock formations that look like short versions of Bryce Caynon's dramatic spires. from a distance they looks like a cute colony of goblins or mushrooms made of rock. But why would you want to come? Well.....

  • They are an internatinally recognized dark sky park (hello stargazing on steroids)
  • Great hikes! They even have some slot cayons.
  • Seriosly how aren't these little guys cute?
